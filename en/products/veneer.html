BINTANGOR VENEER PLYWOOD

Bintangor veneer plywood offers a natural, warm wood appearance with soft grain patterns, making it suitable for residential interiors, showrooms, and applications that require a high level of aesthetics. The Bintangor veneer delivers a premium yet approachable look, which is why it is widely favored by architects and interior designers.

1. Characteristics of Bintangor Veneer

Bintangor veneer features a golden-brown to warm brown tone and is highly receptive to PU coating and surface finishing. Its smooth and balanced grain structure provides a more refined appearance compared to many standard veneer types.

2. Key Advantages

  • - High aesthetic value with attractive grain patterns
  • - Suitable for various interior design styles
  • - Easy to process and finish
  • - Available in multiple adhesive standards

This makes Bintangor veneer plywood an ideal solution for projects that require both durability and visual appeal.

3. Advantages for Manufacturing and Export

Thanks to its wide availability, Bintangor veneer plywood can be supplied in large volumes with flexible technical specifications. It is well suited for Asian and Middle Eastern markets, where consistent quality, aesthetics, and cost efficiency are key requirements
