OKOUME FACED PLYWOOD

If you are looking for plywood with a bright, smooth surface, excellent paintability, and more premium aesthetics compared to standard veneers, Okoume-faced plywood is an ideal choice. This product is widely preferred in markets such as Japan, Korea, Malaysia, and Singapore thanks to its consistent appearance and lightweight structure.

1. Key Characteristics of Okoume Veneer

Okoume veneer features a light pink to reddish tone, smooth texture, and uniform grain pattern. With minimal surface defects, it offers high aesthetic value and reduces sanding requirements during production. Okoume plywood is highly regarded for its visual consistency and clean finish

2. Advantages of Okoume Plywood for Interior Applications

Its lightweight structure helps reduce load on furniture and interior constructions while lowering transportation costs. Okoume plywood also provides excellent paint and coating adhesion, making it suitable for products requiring bright colors or refined finishes. This veneer type is especially favored in markets with high technical and quality standards.

3. Suitability for Export Markets

Okoume-faced plywood is widely selected across Asian and Middle Eastern markets due to its balanced durability, aesthetic quality, and reasonable pricing. Export orders are commonly produced to E1 / E2 standards, or customized based on specific requirements for thickness, core quality, and moisture content.
